How to make your
Burger

ingredients

Qty

ingredient
1/2 pound ground beef
1 Pinch of salt and pepper
1 tomato, sliced
1/4 onion, sliced
1 Lettuce leaves
2 hamburger buns
optional Cheese
optional Bacon
optional Condiments (e.g., ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

How to make

  • Start by preheating a skillet or grill over medium heat.
  • Form ground beef into a patty about 4-5 inches wide and 1/2 inch thick.
  • Season both sides of the patty with salt and pepper.
  • Place the patty on the skillet or grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  • While the patty is cooking, slice a tomato, onion, and lettuce.
  • Toast the burger bun on the skillet or grill for about 1-2 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  • Assemble the burger by placing the lettuce, tomato, and onion on the bottom half of the bun, followed by the cooked patty.
  • Add your favorite toppings, such as cheese, bacon, or condiments, on top of the patty.
  • Finish by placing the top half of the bun on the burger.
  • Enjoy!
